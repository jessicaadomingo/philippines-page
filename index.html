Mabuhay! (Welcome!)
The Philippines is an archepelago of more than 7000 islands, situated in the south eastern corner of Asia. It neighbors countries such as China, Vietnam, Malaysia, and Indonesia. Home to a diverse group of people, languages, foods, and nature, the Philippines is offers a lot to discover. This page is dedicated towards the exploration of various topics concerning the Philippines.
The country itself can be divided into 3 main land masses: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindinao.

Luzon

The upper region of the Philippines. This upper region holds our capital city, Manila.

Visayas

The middle region of the Philippines, also referred to as Central Philippines. It is home to many well-known tourist destinations, such as Boracay Beach and Cebu City.

Mindinao

The lower region of the Philippines. Mindinao is the only muslim-majority region in the Philippines.

Random Facts

  • The Philippines was named after King Philip II of Spain in the early 1500's
  • The Philippines is the only Christian majority country in Asia
  • However, Islam was actually the first religion to reach the Philippines via relations with Muslim traders, before Spanish colonial times
  • The Filipino national animal is the carabao (water buffalo)
  • When in war, the Filipino flag is hung with the red stripe facing upwards; when in peace, it is hung with the blue stripe facing upwards
  • The Filipino flag is the only flag to change meaning depending on its orientation
  • The Filipino currency is the piso
  • The country contains over 7000 islands
